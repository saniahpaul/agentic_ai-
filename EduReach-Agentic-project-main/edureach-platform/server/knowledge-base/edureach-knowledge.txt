BRANCH RECOMMENDATION BASED ON KCET RANK
If student KCET rank is between 1 and 6000: Best branch is AI and Data Science. Also eligible for CSE, IT, ECE, ME, CE under government quota.
If student KCET rank is between 6001 and 8000: Best branch is Computer Science Engineering (CSE). Also eligible for IT, ECE, ME, CE.
If student KCET rank is between 8001 and 10000: Best branch is Information Technology (IT). Also eligible for ECE, ME, CE.
If student KCET rank is between 10001 and 15000: Best branch is Electronics and Communication (ECE). Also eligible for ME, CE.
If student KCET rank is between 15001 and 25000: Best branch is Mechanical Engineering (ME). Also eligible for CE.
If student KCET rank is between 25001 and 35000: Best branch is Civil Engineering (CE).
If student KCET rank is above 35000: Student is not eligible for government quota. They can apply for management quota seats. Management quota has separate fees.

12TH GRADE SCORE GUIDANCE
For B.Tech admission, student must have passed 12th grade with Physics, Chemistry and Mathematics (PCM).
Minimum 12th grade percentage for government quota through KCET: 45 percent in PCM (40 percent for SC and ST students).
Minimum 12th grade percentage for management quota: 60 percent in PCM.
If student scored above 90 percent in 12th grade: They likely have a good KCET rank. Recommend CSE or AI and DS.
If student scored between 75 and 90 percent in 12th grade: They likely qualify for CSE, IT or ECE through KCET.
If student scored between 60 and 75 percent in 12th grade: They may qualify for ECE, ME or CE through KCET or can apply for management quota.
If student scored below 60 percent in 12th grade: They should apply for management quota. KCET eligibility requires minimum 45 percent.

HOW AI COUNSELOR SHOULD HELP STUDENTS
Step 1: Ask the student their name and preferred program (B.Tech, M.Tech, MBA).
Step 2: For B.Tech ask their KCET rank. For M.Tech ask their PGCET rank. Also ask their 12th grade percentage.
Step 3: Based on KCET rank recommend the best branch they can get under government quota.
Step 4: If student asks about a specific branch they want, check if their rank qualifies under government quota. If not, explain management quota option with fees.
Step 5: Ask for their email address to send a detailed personalized summary.
Step 6: Collect their phone number if not already available.
Step 7: Send a warm, helpful summary covering branch recommendation, fee details, eligibility, and next steps.

ADMISSIONS PROCESS
B.Tech: 70 percent seats through KCET counseling (government quota). 30 percent management quota based on 12th marks and interview. Eligibility is 10 plus 2 with PCM minimum 45 percent. JEE Main holders get preference in management quota.
M.Tech: 50 percent through PGCET score. 50 percent through college entrance test. Eligibility is B.Tech or BE with minimum 60 percent.
MBA: 70 percent through KMAT or PGCET counseling. 30 percent management quota. Eligibility is Bachelors degree with minimum 50 percent.
Application opens March 1st. Last date for management quota July 15th. Classes begin August 1st odd semester, January 2nd even semester.
